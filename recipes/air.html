A recipe for breathing air

Man's gotta breathe, right?

It's that time again. You feel the need to inhale, but you're not quite sure how to get yourself a bit of air to breathe. In this recipe, we'll take a look at my own favourite air, the oxygenated kind.

Here's what you'll be needing:

  • A body in need of respiration
  • Available and suitable atmosphere
  • A desire to inhale and/or exhale

All set? Great, follow these next steps:

  1. Bring your body into suitable atmosphere
  2. Double-check body is ready to respirate
  3. Exhale
  4. Inhale

That's it, you have now swapped the oxygen in your body for new, fresh oxygen from the atmosphere!
