Worst hotdog ever?

The following is a sarcastic, not even remotely worthwhile recipe that we here at Superglizzy Industries put together for your reading pleasure. This ultimate glizzy, sometimes referred to as hotdogs, is sure to make your hootenanny the talk of the town as each individual hotdog disappoints your guests in a spectacular and fun way. So, let's get right to it!

Ingredients:

  • One hotdog, cheapest option available
  • One hotdog bun, left out to dry overnight
  • Unusually sweet tomato ketchup
  • Mustard, of the bland variety

Steps:

  1. Put a medium pan on the stove on medium heat
  2. Sprinkle in some olive oil
  3. When ready, put your singular hotdog in the pan and let fry for 5 minutes
  4. Put hotdog in bun
  5. Add ketchup and mustard to taste

It's as simple as that! Always remember, the most disappointing hotdog is the one you can't have!
